Game Rules

1) Click the donut as fast as you can to make as many donuts as you can!
2) If your "Donuts Made" count is greater than 100, you can now purchase your first AutoClicker
3) An AutoClicker will automatically click for us every second, so we don't have to click manually
4) AutoClicker's apply one click per second for every AutoClicker purchased (ex. 1st auto clicker = 1 clicks/second, 2nd auto clicker = 2 clicks/second, 3rd auto clicker = 3 clicks/second, etc...)
5) Each time you purchase an AutoClicker, the cost to purchase will increase by 10 donuts (ex. 1st auto clicker = 100 donuts, 2nd auto clicker = 110 donuts, 3rd auto clicker = 120 donuts, etc...)
6) Once you purchase an AutoClicker, your "Donuts Made" count will decrease by the amount the AutoClicker costs
7) If you do not have enough "Donuts Made" to purchase an AutoClicker, we cleverly greyed out and disabled the BUY button until you do
8) If your "Donuts Made" count is greater than 10, you can now buy your first DonutMultiplier
9) A DonutMultiplier will increase the number of donuts made for us each time we click
10) Donut Multipliers increase the value of a click by 20% for every Donut Multiplier purchased (ex. 1st donut multiplier = 1.2 donuts earned/click, 2nd donut multiplier = 1.44 donuts earned/click, 3rd donut multiplier = 1.728 donuts earned/click)
11) Each time you purchase a DonutMultiplier, the cost to purchase will increase by 10% (ex. 1st donut multiplier = 10 donuts, 2nd donut multiplier = 11 donuts, 3rd donut multiplier = 12.1 donuts, etc...)
12) Once you purchase a Donut Multiplier, your "Donuts Made" count will decrease by the amount the Donut Multiplier costs
13) If you do not have enough "Donuts Made" to purchase a Donut Multiplier, we cleverly (yet again) greyed out and disabled the BUY button until you do
14) Lastly, if you want to reset the game so your coworkers won't know how much time you wasted on clicking a button for the past hour...simply click the "GAME RESET" at the top of our game rules page
